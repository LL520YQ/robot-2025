CALL FOR PAPERS

Overview

The 2025 World Robot Conference(WRC) is the premier event in the field of robotics, providing an international platform for researchers, engineers, and industry professionals. It is dedicated to showcasing the latest innovations in robotics technology, fostering in-depth collaboration between academia and industry, and promoting the application and development of robotics across various fields. The theme of this conference is “Intelligent Integration: Empowering the Future World with Robotics,” which aims to explore the cutting-edge trends in robotics technology and contribute to the building of an intelligent society.
